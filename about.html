Who Am I?

I am a Junior at North Dakota State University (NDSU), pursuing a degree in Software Engineering with a minor in Computer Science. I was born and raised in Fargo, ND, and have always been passionate about computers and coding. Fun fact: I’m part of a set of triplets!

Professional Background

I currently work at Scheels Sporting Goods as a Loss Prevention Investigator, ensuring store security and analyzing footage to prevent losses.

Previously, I worked as a Cyber Security Researcher at NDSU (May 2023 – May 2024), where I focused on identifying network vulnerabilities, analyzing potential threats, and developing digital security solutions.

I am actively seeking a software engineering or related internship for the upcoming summer to further apply my technical skills.

Interests

Outside of academics and work, I train in martial arts (Jiu-Jitsu and Muay Thai), love hiking and camping, and enjoy reading. I enjoy playing video games and watching movies in my free time. One of my favorite movies is Anchorman.
